Our Services

Garments

We specialize in the mass production of garments such as kimonos, ponchos, dresses, tops etc These garments are available in solid colors , printed and embroidered designs

Hand Bags

Short of storage? Here comes the handbag to save the day keep your essentials with you safely in these cotton and jute printed bags with you. Strong material and the fabulous colors.

Custom products

We also produce custom products like tailored designs, embroidery, Colorful tassels, flip flops and even custom made fabric which suits you.
